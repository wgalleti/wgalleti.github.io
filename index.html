Important projects

During my career, some projects were very important, both for professional growth and personal fulfillment.

RFID

In the RFID project, developed during 2014, the company Khulman performed the classification (strength, length, color of the fiber) of the plume samples, and the volume of samples, per harvest, exceeded 250 thousand units. To improve the flow within the laboratory, it would be necessary for the samples to arrive labeled by the customer / producer. For this, we developed a workflow of how the operation would be used and solved numerous internal problems with this project. A single thing that hindered us was the cost of rfid tags, which at the time was almost $ 1 per unit. In this operation, we needed 3 tags, multiplied by 250 thousand samples, the cost was high. Khulman did an impressive job, and participating in this project was a unique experience.
